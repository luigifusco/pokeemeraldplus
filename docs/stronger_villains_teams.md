# Stronger Villains — Team Comparison

Original (vanilla) Team Aqua / Team Magma rosters vs. the **Stronger Villains** mod rosters.

- **Bosses** (Archie, Maxie) and **minibosses** (Matt, Shelly, Tabitha) fight at the gym-leader level for their area, with larger, mostly-evolved teams drawn from a wide thematic palette.
- **Grunts** keep their vanilla levels but each Pokémon is evolved to its level-appropriate form, and every grunt fields at least two Pokémon — three for late-game base grunts (Aqua/Magma Hideout, Seafloor Cavern, Space Center).
- Grunt padding is drawn from a level-gated, per-trainer-rotated pool, so Mightyena/Golbat are no longer piled onto every grunt; they only appear where a grunt's actual vanilla Pokémon is a Poochyena/Zubat.
- Villains are identified by trainer class (Team Aqua/Magma, Aqua/Magma Admin/Leader), so non-villain NPCs that merely share a name (e.g. the Route 108 Swimmer "Matthew") are not affected.
- Rows are ordered by story progression; each grunt row is a separate battle.
